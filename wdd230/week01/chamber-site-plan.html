Our Purpose

The sunset harbor chamber of commerce is committed to the prosperity and success of our community. We work to preserve and promote the unique nature and culture of our community for generations to come.
We are the Sunset Harbor Chamber of Commerce, a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting and supporting the local economy. We serve as a voice for the community and facilitate connections between businesses, residents and visitors. We believe in our community and want to do all we can to make it a better place for our residents, visitors and local businesses alike.
We are here to give you the tools, resources, and connections you need to take your business to the next level. And we want you to be a part of it!
